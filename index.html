About

TagoBeats - Robin Busse

tagobeats.com

I always loved music, played instruments and was hooked on the idea of how music actually gets produced. I dived into it and never really stopped. What began as self-taught beatmaking turned into a Bachelor of Engineering in Audio Engineering at Hochschule Mittweida, and eventually my own studio just outside Leipzig.

Seven years and 300+ tracks later, I still chase that same feeling, mostly in hip-hop and trap, but never saying no to a genre I haven't tried yet. Full production, mixing, mastering, engineering: whatever it takes to get a track sounding exactly the way it should.

That engineering background pulled me past the DAW too. I'm now programming my own VST plugins and DSP algorithms, building the exact tools I couldn't find off the shelf.
