What it does

The exact FL curve

The Fruity Soft Clipper transfer curve, identified by measurement down to float noise. FL mode is a 1:1 clone, plus hard and tanh curves on top.

Oversampling

The original aliases. TagoClip ships with 4x and 8x oversampling to clean that up, or leave it off for the honest 1:1 sound.

Delta listening

One button plays only what gets clipped away, so you hear exactly what you are trading for loudness.

Mono low end

Sum everything below a chosen frequency to mono and keep the 808 centered, right inside the clipper.
