What it does

Pitch, ±12 semitones

Shift a vocal a full octave up or down. Per-semitone level compensation keeps the output volume steady while you sweep.

Independent formant

Move the character without moving the pitch, or fix the character while the pitch shifts. That is what keeps big shifts sounding like a voice.

Mix and gain

Blend the shifted signal against the dry vocal for instant parallel layers, with output gain to slot it into the mix.

Presets

Deep voice, octave up and more, one click away. Good starting points for ad-libs, doubles and pitched hooks.
