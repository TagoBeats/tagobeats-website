How licensing works

Licensing a TagoBeats instrumental takes a few minutes: pick a beat in the player, choose a license tier on BeatStars and check out there. Lease licenses cover streaming releases and come with untagged WAV and MP3 files, higher tiers add trackout stems, and exclusive rights take the beat off the market entirely. For custom production or questions about a specific beat, reach out at music@tagobeats.com.
